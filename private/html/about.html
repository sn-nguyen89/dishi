Our Story

We're a group of CST students who are all foodies at heart and passionate about making a positive impact on our city. When we found out that 31% of landfill waste comes from food, we were inspired to come up with a solution. Dishi was created to divert excess and leftover food from vendors to buyers. We will help local businesses turn a profit on food that would have otherwise gone to waste, and allow our users to access inexpensive food options while feeling good knowing that they're helping the environment. We're excited to apply our coding knowledge and turn our vision into reality, and we hope to see others join us in our adventure to create a more sustainable planet. This is our mission: saving the planet, one dish at a time.
